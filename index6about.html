My Gold Award Project!

Me

Hi! My name is Emily Cronin, and I am an incoming high school senior working on my Girl Scout Gold Award Project. I have been
a Girl Scout since kindergarten, and am so excited to finally share this project with others. Composting, recycling,
and reusing are all important to my life, and I hope they can now be important to yours too!

My Project

My goal for this project is to educate my community about the three R's: reducing, reusing, and recycling.
So far I've worked with my local library and elementary school to complete this goal. Now you are accessing
this website my sister and I created in hope of educating elementary students about what you can compost, recycle,
and make using reusable items. We hope you enjoyed it and learned useful facts!!
